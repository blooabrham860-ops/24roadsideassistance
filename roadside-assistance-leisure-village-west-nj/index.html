Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services are included in basic roadside assistance?

It usually covers tire changes, battery jumps, lockouts, and fuel delivery. Some plans add towing up to a certain distance.

2. How soon can roadside help arrive?

In cities, expect 15-30 minutes. In remote areas, it might take 45-60 minutes. We send updates via app or text.

3. Does roadside assistance help with flat tires?

Yes, we can repair or replace the spare if you have one. For bigger damage, we arrange a tow to a shop.

4. Is there roadside coverage for motorcycles or RVs?

Standard plans focus on cars, but options like roadside service are made for RVs and bikes with special tools.

Leisure Village West, NJ Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Leisure Village West is an unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) located within Manchester Township, in Ocean County, in the U.S. state of New Jersey. Until the 2000 census, the CDP had been part of the Leisure Village West-Pine Lake Park CDP, which was split for the 2010 enumeration into separate CDPs for Leisure Village West and Pine Lake Park. As of the 2010 United States Census, the CDP's population was 3,493. Leisure Village West is one of several active adult communities bearing similar names. Leisure Village and Leisure Village East are the other two communities nearby.
